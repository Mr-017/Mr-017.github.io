腾讯视频
腾讯视频于2011年4月正式上线，现以“不负好时光” 品牌理念，以更加年轻化、更能引起用户情感共鸣的定位全新亮相，并展现了全新品牌标识。

Tencent video was officially launched in April 2011. Now, with the brand concept of "live up to the good time", Tencent video has made a brand new appearance with the positioning of being younger and more able to arouse users' emotional resonance, and shows a brand new brand identity.


爱奇艺视频
爱奇艺视频于2010年4月22日成立, 以“悦享品质”为公司口号，基于“屏”充分体现多屏合一和网络视频的互动特性。

Iqiyi video was founded on April 22, 2010, with the slogan of "enjoy quality" and based on "screen", fully reflecting the interactive characteristics of multi screen integration and network video.





优酷视频
优酷于2006年6月21日正式上线，以“这世界很酷”为口号，以“让世界尽享快乐、智慧和感动”作为使命。

Youku was officially established on June 21, 2006, with the slogan of "the world is cool" and the mission of "let the world enjoy happiness, wisdom and emotion".
